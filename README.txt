ANKITA SHARMA ONLINE PORTFOLIO

FILES
- index.html: the website
- styles.css: design and layout
- script.js: animations
- assets/: your photo, resume, campaign deck and portfolio PDF

HOW TO PREVIEW
Open index.html in Chrome or Safari.

HOW TO PUBLISH FREE ON NETLIFY
1. Go to https://app.netlify.com/drop
2. Drag the entire ankita-sharma-portfolio folder into the page.
3. Netlify will publish it and give you a temporary web address.
4. You can then connect ankitasharma.in under Domain Settings.

HOW TO CONNECT YOUR DOMAIN
Buy ankitasharma.in from a domain provider such as GoDaddy, Namecheap or Hostinger.
Then follow Netlify's DNS instructions.
